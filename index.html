When we review these recommendations at the beginning of each tax year, we make some assumptions around your workplace pension, employment situation, and pension carry forward. It's especially important to check these assumptions are correct if you have the following, otherwise our recommendations may not be suitable for you:

  • have an income of more than £175,000 per year
or
  • think your savings toward retirement could be more than £30,000 this tax year (including any employer contributions and any tax relief)
or
  • have an active defined benefit pension
